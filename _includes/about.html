About Plotypus

Plotypus is a team devoted to innovation for authors and writers. We want to build tools to make the writing process more cohesive and easier, facilitating the entire creative process from the first concepts of your brave new world to the final publication.

We formed as a senior project in the final year of the pursuit of our degrees in computer science at the University of Utah.

Our first project together is InkWeaver, a new environment for writing and keeping notes on your characters, events, and other ideas. If you're interested in writing stories, be sure to check it out!
