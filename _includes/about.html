About Plotypus

Plotypus is a team devoted to innovation for authors of all varieties. We want to build tools to make the writing process more cohesive and easier, facilitating the entire creative process from the first fledgling concepts in your mind to the final publication of your brave new world.

We formed as a capstone project group in the final year of our undergraduate degrees in computer science at the University of Utah.

Our first project together is InkWeaver, an intelligent writing environment designed to help connect your story to all the notes about your characters, locations, events, and more, while simultaneously showing you statistics of your writing along the way. If you have any interest in writing, you should check it out!
